Peachtree City Chamber of Commerce

Peachtree City is the largest city in Fayette County, Georgia, United States. As of the 2010 United States Census, it had a population of 34,364. Peachtree City is located in South Metro Atlanta. Peachtree City is noted for its extensive use of golf carts.

Purpose

The Peachtree City Chamber of Commerce website serves as a platform for members of the chamber of commerce and the community to promote their business and highlight the attractions and events in the city. It also provides business resources, networking opportunities, and advocacy on behalf of the community.

Color Schema: The color scheme will be based on the colors of the Peachtree City logo, which include shades of green, pink, yellow, and orange. These colors will be used throughout the website to maintain consistency and reinforce the identity of the city. Typography: The website will use modern and clean fonts, such as Roboto and Open Sans, for headings and body text. These fonts are easy to read and are widely supported by web browsers. Graphic Identity: The website will feature high-quality images of the city's attractions and events. These images will be used throughout the website to showcase the city's unique features and to provide a visual representation of the community. Content: The website will provide a range of content, including information about the chamber of commerce, its members, events, and resources. It will also feature news and updates related to the city's business community and a comprehensive business directory. Additionally, the website will highlight the city's attractions, accommodations, and restaurants to promote tourism in the area. Responsive Design: The website will be designed to be responsive, which means it will adjust its layout based on the device being used to access it. This will ensure that the website is accessible and easy to use on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

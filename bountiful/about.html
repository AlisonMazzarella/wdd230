About Us

Bountiful Foods is a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, but we also have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.

As a company, we sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in our area, but we also work with national and international suppliers to offer a wider range of options. We cater to walk-in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, through our food trucks.

We deliver and manage orders both over the phone and via our website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

Sustainability is increasingly important to us, which is why we invest in hydroponic agriculture.

We only source seasonal produce, so you get the best flavor.

We believe in supporting local family farms right here in California.

When we do source nationally and internationally, we use fuel-efficient, eco-friendly transportation.

Events are a favorite of ours. We love teaching people how to cook fresh, local, and seasonal food.

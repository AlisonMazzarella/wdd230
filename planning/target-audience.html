Purpose Target Audience Design

Target Audience

Business owners: Business owners in Peachtree City who are interested in joining the chamber of commerce and accessing the benefits and resources offered by the organization.

Local residents: Residents of Peachtree City who want to learn more about the city's businesses and events.

Personas

Alice - Business Owner

Alice is a local business owner in Peachtree City who recently opened a new bakery in town. She wants to network with other business owners in the community and access resources that can help her grow her business. Alice is in her late 30s and has a busy schedule, so she needs a website that is easy to navigate and provides quick access to information.

David - Tourist

David is a tourist visiting Peachtree City for the first time. He wants to explore the city's attractions, restaurants, and accommodations. David is in his early 50s and is tech-savvy, so he expects a website that is user-friendly and provides accurate information.

5 Scenarios

Sarah wants to become a member of the chamber of commerce and access the benefits and resources offered by the organization. She visits the website and navigates to the Membership page to learn about the different membership levels and their corresponding benefits.

Alex is looking for information about the city's upcoming events. He visits the website and navigates to the Events page, where he finds a calendar of upcoming events with detailed information about each one.

Barbara is interested in networking with other business owners in the community. She visits the website and navigates to the Business Directory page, where she can search for other members by industry or business name.

John is looking for a place to stay during his visit to Peachtree City. He visits the website and navigates to the Accommodations page, where he finds a list of hotels and rental properties with information about their amenities and rates.

Julie wants to learn about the different resources available to members of the chamber of commerce. She visits the website and navigates to the Resources page, where she finds information about business planning, marketing, and financing.

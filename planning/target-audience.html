Purpose Target Audience Design

Target Audience

Health Lovers: Individuals who prioritize their well-being and seek to maintain a healthy lifestyle through nutritious choices. They value organic, locally-sourced ingredients and are interested in learning about products and services that align with their health goals.

Organic Businesses: Establishments that focus on providing organic products or services. These businesses prioritize sustainability, eco-friendly practices, and promoting a healthy lifestyle. They may include organic farms, food vendors, wellness centers, or sustainable retailers.

Personas

Healthy Hannah Age: 32 Occupation: Yoga instructor and wellness enthusiast Background: Hannah is passionate about living a healthy and balanced lifestyle. She values organic and locally-sourced products and actively seeks out businesses that align with her health-conscious mindset.

Busy Brian Age: 50 Occupation: Corporate executive and health-conscious professional Background: Brian leads a fast-paced lifestyle with demanding work responsibilities. He understands the importance of eating healthy but struggles to find time for meal planning and preparation.

Scenarios

Healthy Lunch Break by the Coast: John, a tourist visiting Carlsbad, California, spends his day exploring the coastal area. As lunchtime approaches, he craves a fresh and healthy meal. He spots one of Bountiful Foods' food trucks conveniently parked near the beach and decides to order a delicious salad made with locally grown, organic ingredients. John is delighted to experience the bounty of the region and indulge in a satisfying, nutritious lunch while enjoying the coastal ambiance.

Farmers Market Discovery: Sarah, a local resident of Carlsbad, loves to support local businesses and eat fresh, organic produce. On a weekend, she visits the Carlsbad Farmers Market, where she discovers the vibrant booth of Bountiful Foods. Sarah engages in conversation with the friendly staff, learns about the local farmers they work with, and purchases a variety of seasonal fruits and vegetables. She feels a connection to the community and appreciates the convenience of accessing high-quality, locally sourced produce.

Online Ordering for Home Delivery: Michael, a busy professional residing in Carlsbad, wants to maintain a healthy lifestyle despite his hectic schedule. He visits Bountiful Foods' website and discovers the convenience of online ordering and home delivery. Michael customizes a fruit bag filled with an assortment of fresh, organic fruits and places an order. He appreciates the ease of the process and eagerly awaits the doorstep delivery, knowing he can enjoy nourishing and bountiful fruits without compromising his busy routine.

Catering a Wellness Event: Bountiful Foods receives a request to cater a wellness event at a coastal retreat center. The event focuses on promoting healthy eating and a holistic lifestyle. The owner-operator of Bountiful Foods collaborates with the organizers to curate a menu showcasing a variety of organic, locally grown dishes, including vibrant salads, nourishing bowls, and refreshing fruit drinks. The participants at the event savor the bountiful and flavorful offerings, feeling energized and inspired to continue their wellness journey.

Walk-In at the Carlsbad Storefront: Emily, a Carlsbad local, decides to take a leisurely stroll along the streets of downtown Carlsbad. As she walks past Bountiful Foods' storefront, she is enticed by the aroma of fresh produce and the colorful display of fruits. Curious, she steps inside and discovers a wide range of organic offerings, from ripe berries to crisp vegetables. Emily engages in a conversation with the knowledgeable staff, seeking advice on creating a healthy meal. She leaves the store with a bounty of nutritious ingredients, excited to embark on her culinary adventure.
